Photo 101: 6/6

The Idea

Presented by Dane Aleksander, art director at Animat Habitat.

Continued from The Personality. Part of the Animat Habitat studio workshop: Character Design for Animation.

Does the character have a purpose in life, and what is it? – Does the character want something more than anything, and how would the character react if that was threatened? – Does the character have a best and worst memory, what are they, and how does the character react when asked about one or the other? – What is the character most afraid of, and what would the character do to prevent that from happening? – What does the character know that no one else does? – Who knows the character best? – How has family shaped the character, and does being around them make the character more or less true to oneself? – If the character could live anywhere, where would it be, and what does this place mean to the character? – Does the character have a favorite attire, and is fashion important to the character? – How have the times shaped the character? – What key features of the character have been defined by the time? – And how does age define the character?
