Photo 101: 2/6

The Character

Presented by Dane Aleksander, art director at Animat Habitat.

Continued from The Audience. Part of the Animat Habitat studio workshop: Character Design for Animation.

We focus on the key features of a character design that help to reveal the identify of a character and the role of the character in a story. We then exaggerate these features to make a character appear larger than life. We see that an accessory, an attire, a change in appearance can serve as an extension of character, and how this can help to bring out the back-story as well as to symbolize the story arc of a character.
