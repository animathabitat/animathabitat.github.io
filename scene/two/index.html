White Tiger is an oil painting of an Amur tiger in the taiga. This painting is photographed and reproduced in limited edition as an archival pigment print on acid-free archival paper, and printed with an inch unprinted matte to allow for safe handling as well as for framing options. The archival pigment prints are available in two sizes. A digital copy of the corrected and edited photograph is also available. Les tirages pigmentaires d'archives sont disponibles en deux tailles. Une copie numérique de la photo corrigée et éditée est également disponible.
