Making of Ella Ch. 3: Animatic

Dane Aleksander, animator at Animat Habitat

This is an animatic — a pre-visualization of a visual story. The focus is placed on pacing and on a linear sequence of events. This is the White Elephant animatic, with comic book pages and panels fitted to the standard widescreen format. This working timeline for the story serves as a foundation for cinematography and audio engineering and so on.
