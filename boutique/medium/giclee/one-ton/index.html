“One Ton” is one of a collection of digital paintings painted in the making of Last of the Big Tuskers (documentary film, 2018). This digital painting is printed in limited edition as an archival pigment print on acid-free archival paper, and printed with a half-inch unprinted matte to allow for safe handling as well as for framing options. The archival pigment prints are available in two sizes. \ « One Ton » fait partie d'une collection de peintures numériques peintes lors de la réalisation du film documentaire Last of the Big Tuskers.
